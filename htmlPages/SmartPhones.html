0.5 JNCD

Just noticeable color difference (JNCD) is a measure of color accuracy. A score of less than 1 is indistinguishable by the human eye. Each GT 2 Pro undergoes a color accuracy check during production to ensure color accuracy is kept to 0.5, visually indistinguishable from perfect.

1.07 billion colors

This 10-bit display is able to reproduce over 1.07 billion different colors, that’s 64 times more than 8-bit screens. The P3 color gamut is much wider than the standard sRGB. This means the Super Reality display is able to produce a far greater range of colors. If you want to enjoy HDR content properly, you need 100% P3 coverage.

5,000,000:1 contrast ratio

Contrast ratio measures the difference between the brightest whites, and the darkest blacks. The higher the number, the better the image. With a whopping 5 million to 1 ratio, you’ll get brighter whites, and darker blacks creating a more vibrant and rich viewing experience.

Back

1-120Hz dynamic refresh rate

Intelligently adjusts the refresh rate based on what's on screen, anywhere from 1-120Hz. Compared with standard 120Hz screens, its power consumption is reduced by up to 50%, giving you up to 1.7 hours longer battery life.

1000Hz turbocharged touch sampling

Super reality display combines the most advanced display hardware and software optimization to give you lightning-fast responsiveness.This result is a boost in touch sampling rate to a whopping 1,000hz.
